Equations as stated by Shao and Lister(1996)
n Heat conduction eq.
u Cm = heat capacity
u k = conductivity
u T = temperature at time, t and depth, z.
n Energy balance eq.
u S = solar irradience
u a = albedo
u L’ = net longwave irradience
u H = sensible heat flux density
u LE = latent heat flux density
u G = ground conductive heat flux density
